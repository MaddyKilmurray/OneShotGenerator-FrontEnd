D&D 5E One Shot Generator

Fans probably know this better than anyone, but one of the major downfalls of Dungeons & Dragons is just how much time it can take to finish a campaign. For those who have dense schedules, kids, or other obligations, this kind of time might seem impossible to come by. Fortunately, there are still ways to play D&D, even with a strict schedule...

By playing a D&D One Shot!

A one shot is a single, short session, and can be played as an indepent game, or as part of a larger campaign.

Generate everything that you need for a quick oneshot with our D&D 5E One Shot generator. Players can randomly generate their characters, and Dungeon Masters can create a party to bring all the characters together.
